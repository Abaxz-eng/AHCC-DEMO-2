About us

Built for healthier animals and stronger farms.

We provide practical education, field-ready advisory services, and farm management systems for livestock producers at every stage.

Mission

To equip farmers with reliable livestock skills, animal healthcare knowledge, and farm systems that improve productivity.

Vision

To become a trusted centre for livestock training, animal health consultancy, and sustainable farm development.

Core Values

Professionalism, practical learning, animal welfare, integrity, biosecurity, and measurable farm improvement.

Why Choose AHC?

  • Hands-on livestock training
  • Health-focused farm support
  • Beginner-friendly and commercial guidance
  • Consultancy for setup, scaling, and disease control
